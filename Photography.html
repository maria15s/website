All About Photography!

For two years now I have been interested in not just taking photos but also learning more about how it all started. The history of photography is just as interesting as learning different techniques with a camera. I am going to start to dive into some histroy about cameras and how it all started.

The Beginning

Photography began in the late 1830's, starting in France. Joseph nicéphore Niépce used a camera obscura to expose a pewter plate coated with bitumen to light. This was the first image that didn't fade quickly. His success led to a number of other people's experiments and then photography started to progress faster.
